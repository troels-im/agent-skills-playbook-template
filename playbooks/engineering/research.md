# Research

Use this playbook when a technical, product, legal, security, or dependency
question requires external or source-backed research.

## Workflow

1. State the research question.
2. Prefer primary sources and official documentation.
3. Record dates for information that may change.
4. Save findings under the work trace or docs area.
5. Separate evidence from recommendation.
6. Link findings to requirements, ADRs, or tickets they affect.

## Verification

- Findings cite sources.
- Recommendations trace to evidence.
- `just test` passes when repository files change.
